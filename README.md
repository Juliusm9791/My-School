## Description

The My School App is the new all-in-one place to communicate with your school! Between faculty, parents and students, My School makes it easy to post an announcement and have your message heard across the halls. Create an account today to begin engaging with all the activity going on across departments and grade levels. Declare your post as an event, and share up to five pictures per announcement. Users can easily edit or delete their posts anytime, and can quickly search or filter the content. Upload your avatar and letâ€™s make a better school today!
